Institute Email Account

BITS Pilani provides you with a (Google G-Suite based) email address @bitspilani-digital.edu.in during all your studies at BITS. You should have already received your email login credentials before starting this Orientation Course.

To access your email, please open Gmail in your browser and log in using the full email address. You can also use email client software like Outlook or Thunderbird.

All staff and professors of BITS Pilani will use this email address to communicate with you.

Note: It is extremely important that you check your BITS Pilani email daily. All correspondence regarding the courses and official updates will be sent to your @bitspilani-digital.edu.in email address only.

Please note that your account storage space comprises "Gmail + Google Drive + Google Photos".

To check your current usage, click on the "Storage" menu option in Google Drive. It will show the storage space used for each of the applications, i.e., Gmail, Google Drive, and Google Photos separately along with the total storage space used.

You are requested to keep total account storage usage to less than 15 GB else you risk losing your data.
